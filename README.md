**Hey, I’m Junior.** ☕️
I’ve spent the last 25 years living in that quiet space between pixels and people, architecting the parts of software that users feel before they understand why. Brand. Interaction. Emotional weight. The controlled power that only real experience can deliver.

**The Grind & The Heavy Lifting**
My career didn’t start at the top. I cut my teeth starting in 1999, pushing pixels across San Francisco and New York, putting in the reps before I ever stepped foot into 2Advanced Studios in 2005. There, I came in as Senior Art Director and left as Executive Creative Director almost nine years later, helping define what the interactive web could be when the rest of the world was still figuring out how to load a page.

From there, I spent a decade doing the heavy lifting under NDAs. I co-founded Bad Juju Brands, led product and creative at Ozobot (Marvel, Disney STEM robotics) and Action Face (NBA 3D avatars), and served as Head of Product at Freestyle+ (mental fitness games shipped to CES). I’ve curated the invisible layers for Kabam, Pepsi, Nvidia, Warner Bros, Lego, ASUS, Sony, and Activision.

All of it happened while being a single-income provider, raising four kids, and pulling the all-nighters required to execute for the vision while still showing up as Dad.

**The Evolution**
In 2020, I dove headfirst into web3: NFTs, PFPs, community, and decentralization. It felt like 1997 all over again; I was that same kid falling in love with the web for the first time. Then, AI showed up and the circle closed.

**The Legacy Era: Regeneer**
Today, my solopreneur era isn't just about shipping code; it's a family legacy.
Five years ago, my father (Elder Jerez Sr., a visionary architect with 36 years of design-build execution) and I founded Regeneer. After weathering the pandemic, we have spent the last two years building a lean, post-AI startup for the regenerative industry, without the VC strings.

Regeneer is the collision of two superpowers: Sr.’s physical architecture and my non-conforming futurist tech. We are building "Worlds and Stories", bridging digital ecosystems with physical outdoor destinations, because we believe regeneration scales through experience, not lectures.

I handle the digital stack solo. Idea → design → brand → code → marketing → shipped product. I am leveraging the AI tools of today to engineer the exact types of products I used to lead massive teams to create.

I’m still that kid who tinkers, who stays early, and who refuses to quit. Welcome to my digital workshop, where the guy who used to curate the magic for everyone else finally gets to build it for his own legacy.

One honest day at a time.
